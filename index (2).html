— The Art of the Pour —

Signature Cocktails

Crafted by the world's leading bartenders, exclusively for Jessep.

🥃

The Obsidian Old Fashioned

The gold standard, elevated. Jessep's smoky depth meets the warmth of demerara and a single large ice sphere.

  1. Muddle 2 dashes Angostura bitters with 1 tsp demerara syrup
  2. Add 60ml Jessep 18 Year Reserve over ice
  3. Stir 30 seconds until perfectly chilled
  4. Strain into rocks glass over large ice sphere
  5. Express orange peel, garnish and serve
🍸

Midnight Negroni

A bold reimagining. Jessep Midnight Cognac XO transforms the classic into something hauntingly beautiful.

  1. Combine 30ml Jessep Midnight Cognac, 30ml Campari, 30ml sweet vermouth
  2. Stir with ice for 45 seconds — no shaking
  3. Strain into chilled coupe glass
  4. Garnish with a charred orange slice
  5. Serve immediately and savour slowly
🌅

Solstice Sour

Light yet complex. The Solstice Single Malt sings in this citrus-forward, honey-kissed classic.

  1. Shake 60ml Jessep Solstice, 25ml fresh lemon juice, 15ml honey syrup
  2. Add egg white if desired; dry shake first, then with ice
  3. Fine strain into chilled sour glass
  4. Garnish with lemon wheel and edible gold
✨

Celestial Highball

The most elevated highball you'll ever encounter. Premium sparkling water meets the Celestial 25 — effortless luxury.

  1. Fill tall glass with large ice cubes — quality matters
  2. Pour 50ml Jessep Celestial 25 Year gently over ice
  3. Top with premium Japanese sparkling water (2:1 ratio)
  4. Stir once, gently, with a long spoon
  5. Garnish with a thin lemon twist and serve
